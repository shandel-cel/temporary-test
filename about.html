About Me

Get to know who I am and what drives my passion for web development

My Story

Hello! I'm Aiza Palin, a passionate and friendly learner currently diving into the world of web development. I love expressing creativity through both code and design, and anime like Solo Leveling inspires the themes of my work.

My journey in web development began during my freshman year at university when I discovered the power of creating interactive experiences through code. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a passion that drives me to continuously learn and improve.

"Creating beautiful and meaningful digital experiences."

What Drives Me

Creativity

I believe that coding is as much an art as it is a science. Finding elegant solutions to complex problems and crafting visually stunning interfaces brings me joy.

Continuous Learning

The web development field is constantly evolving, and I embrace the challenge of staying up-to-date with new technologies and best practices.

User Experience

Creating interfaces that are not only beautiful but also intuitive and accessible is a core principle in all my projects.

Interests & Hobbies

Anime & Manga

I'm an avid anime fan with Solo Leveling being one of my favorites. The art style and storytelling often inspire my design aesthetic.

Digital Art

When I'm not coding, I enjoy creating digital art and experimenting with different styles and techniques.
